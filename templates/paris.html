Top Attractions

Eiffel Tower

The Eiffel Tower is one of the most recognizable landmarks in the world. Built in 1889, it offers stunning views of Paris from its observation decks.

Louvre Museum

The Louvre is the world's largest art museum and home to many famous artworks, including Leonardo da Vinci's Mona Lisa.

Notre-Dame Cathedral

This Gothic masterpiece is a symbol of Paris and a must-see for its incredible architecture and stained-glass windows.
